The Legend Behind Journey Bell

On the road there are stories older than the highway—of road gremlins that rattle bolts loose, foul our luck, and nudge us toward breakdowns. Riders found a simple answer: a small brass bell given in friendship, hung low so its ring keeps the gremlins at bay. The bell works best when it’s a gift.

Journey Bell is our take on that tradition—cast brass for a rich, confident tone, with a form that nods to the classic Harley ride bell. Clip it to bars, bags, or dash; hang it in the RV, Jeep, or garage. Wherever your wheels point, let the bell’s voice say: not today, gremlins.

Gift it to someone you love, or receive one from a fellow traveler. Either way, every ring is a reminder that no one rides alone.
